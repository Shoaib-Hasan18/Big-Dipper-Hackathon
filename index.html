🎯 Hackathon Goals

Our primary goal is to get you comfortable with our codebase and development environment. We want you to have fun exploring and experimenting with our stack!

🔧 Setup & Exploration

Get the codebase set up on your local machine and run the application successfully. Explore the architecture and understand how different components interact.

🧪 Experimentation

Play with the code! Add components, make changes, and see how things work. This is a no-judgment zone - feel free to try anything that interests you.

🤝 Collaboration

Work with the team to understand our development workflow, pull request process, and coding standards. Ask questions and share your ideas!
